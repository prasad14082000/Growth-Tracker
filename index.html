The story

I run 5K every morning
without headphones.
Not for mindfulness.

Because it's the only time I can't escape my own thoughts.

In 2023 I attended a two-month theatre workshop in Pune. I used to run through traffic after office just to reach on time. For the first time in years I felt like myself. Then the workshop ended.

2024 and 2025 were two completely lost years. Exam prep. Job searching. Oscillating between what I was supposed to do and what I actually wanted to do. Every morning I woke up knowing exactly what I wanted. Every morning I chose everything else instead. Cognitive dissonance doesn't feel like conflict. It feels like numbness.

Jan 2026

Stopped the exam prep. Stopped waiting for permission. Started making videos. Started taking photos. Started writing what was actually on my mind.

Mar 2026

Committed to posting every single day for 31 days. Not because I had it figured out. Because I was tired of the numbness.

This website

Is what that looks like in real time.
